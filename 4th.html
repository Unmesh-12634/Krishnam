40 Famous Names of Lord Krishna

1. **Govinda (गोविन्द)** – The protector of cows and Earth
2. **Gopal (गोपाल)** – The caretaker of cows
3. **Keshava (केशव)** – The one with beautiful hair
4. **Madhava (माधव)** – Husband of the Goddess of Fortune
5. **Murari (मुरारी)** – The destroyer of demon Mura
6. **Vasudeva (वासुदेव)** – Son of Vasudeva
7. **Hari (हरि)** – The one who removes sins
8. **Shyam (श्याम)** – The dark-skinned Lord
9. **Jagannatha (जगन्नाथ)** – The Lord of the Universe
10. **Parthasarathi (पार्थसारथी)** – Charioteer of Arjuna
11. **Damodara (दामोदर)** – The Lord tied with a rope by Yashoda
12. **Achyuta (अच्युत)** – The infallible one
13. **Radhanath (राधानाथ)** – The Lord of Radha
14. **Yadunandan (यदुनन्दन)** – Son of the Yadu dynasty
15. **Murlimanohar (मुरलीमनोहर)** – The flute-playing Lord who enchants all
16. **Nanda Kumar (नंदकुमार)** – Son of Nanda
17. **Giridhari (गिरिधारी)** – The lifter of Govardhan Hill
18. **Bihari (बिहारी)** – The playful one
19. **Balagopal (बालगोपाल)** – The divine child Krishna
20. **Madhusudan (मधुसूदन)** – The slayer of the demon Madhu
21. **Rasik (रसिक)** – The supreme enjoyer
22. **Mohan (मोहन)** – The enchanter
23. **Adhokshaja (अधोक्षज)** – Beyond material perception
24. **Vishnu (विष्णु)** – The all-pervading Lord
25. **Satyananda (सत्यानंद)** – The embodiment of eternal bliss
26. **Gyaneshwar (ज्ञानेश्वर)** – The Lord of wisdom
27. **Lila Purushottama (लीला पुरुषोत्तम)** – The divine player
28. **Anantavikrama (अनंतविक्रम)** – The one with infinite power
29. **Ranchod (रणछोड़)** – The one who left the battlefield (for divine play)
30. **Chaturbhuj (चतुर्भुज)** – The four-armed Lord
31. **Karunamayi (करुणामयी)** – The merciful one
32. **Padmanabha (पद्मनाभ)** – The Lord with a lotus-shaped navel
33. **Sarvapalaka (सर्वपालक)** – The protector of all
34. **Shrikrishna (श्रीकृष्ण)** – The all-attractive one
35. **Venuvadan (वेणुवादन)** – The flute player
36. **Pundarikaksha (पुण्डरीकाक्ष)** – Lotus-eyed Lord
37. **Janardana (जनार्दन)** – The one who liberates humanity
38. **Shri Hari (श्रीहरि)** – The supreme remover of obstacles
39. **Rasraj (रसराज)** – The king of divine love
40. **MahaYogi (महायोगी)** – The greatest meditator
