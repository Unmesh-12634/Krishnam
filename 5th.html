Radha Krishna Shlokas & Mantras

1. Krishnaya Vasudevaya Mantra

कृष्णाय वासुदेवाय हरये परमात्मने।
प्रणतः क्लेशनाशाय गोविंदाय नमो नमः॥

Meaning: Salutations to Krishna, the son of Vasudeva, who is the Supreme Soul and removes the sufferings of His devotees.

2. Hare Krishna Mahamantra

हरे कृष्ण हरे कृष्ण कृष्ण कृष्ण हरे हरे
हरे राम हरे राम राम राम हरे हरे॥

Meaning: A powerful mantra that invokes the divine energy of Lord Krishna and Lord Rama for peace, devotion, and liberation.

3. Vasudeva Sutam Devam

वासुदेवसुतं देवं कंसचाणूरमर्दनम्।
देवकीपरमानन्दं कृष्णं वन्दे जगद्गुरुम्॥

Meaning: I bow to Lord Krishna, the son of Vasudeva, the supreme joy of Devaki, and the one who destroyed Kansa and Chanoora.

4. Gita Shloka – Surrender to Krishna

सर्वधर्मान्परित्यज्य मामेकं शरणं व्रज।
अहं त्वां सर्वपापेभ्यो मोक्षयिष्यामि मा शुचः॥

Meaning: Abandon all duties and surrender to Me. I shall free you from all sins. Do not fear.

5. Govinda Damodara Madhaveti

गोविन्द दामोदर माधवेति
हे कृष्ण हे यादव हे सखेति॥

Meaning: Chanting the divine names of Krishna, Govinda, and Madhava brings spiritual joy and removes all difficulties.

6. Achyutam Keshavam

अच्युतं केशवं रामनारायणम्।
कृष्णदामोदरं वासुदेवं हरिम्॥

Meaning: This mantra praises the many names of Lord Krishna, describing His eternal nature and divine presence.

7. Om Namo Bhagavate Vasudevaya

ॐ नमो भगवते वासुदेवाय॥

Meaning: I bow to Lord Vasudeva, the Supreme God, who grants liberation and divine knowledge.

8. Yada Yada Hi Dharmasya

यदा यदा हि धर्मस्य ग्लानिर्भवति भारत।
अभ्युत्थानम् अधर्मस्य तदात्मानं सृजाम्यहम्॥

Meaning: Whenever righteousness declines and unrighteousness rises, I manifest Myself to restore dharma.

9. Madhurashtakam

अधरं मधुरं वदनं मधुरं।
नयनं मधुरं हसितं मधुरम्॥

Meaning: Everything about Lord Krishna is sweet and beautiful, from His lips and smile to His divine presence.

10. Bhaja Govindam

भज गोविन्दं भज गोविन्दं गोविन्दं भज मूढमते।
संप्राप्ते सन्निहिते काले न हि न हि रक्षति डुकृञ्करणे॥

Meaning: Worship Govinda! When the final moment comes, knowledge alone will not save you; devotion is the key.
